> Source of truth: `assets/js/content.js` → `SHOWCASE` array.
> For each new photo, also set the `image:` field in `content.js` (currently `null` for the last three).

**Tips:**
- Shoot against a dark or textured background to complement the Night Forest site theme.
- Export as optimized PNG files and compress them before upload.
- Target **250–450 KB per product image** whenever possible.
- If your export is larger than ~500 KB, run it through a compressor like TinyPNG or Squoosh before replacing the site asset.
- PNG does not use a traditional JPEG-style quality slider, so optimization/compression is the key step.
- The card visuals are `172px` tall with `object-fit: cover`, so centre your subject vertically.
